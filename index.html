I am currently an undergraduate student studying Biology at UC Riverside. I hope to graduate from the B.S. program in 2021. I am an aspiring dentist and am in the middle of studying for the Dental Admissions Test. I will be applying for dental school post graduation in hopes of attending a school in California. I plan to study and specialize in oral surgery in hopes of opening up a practice of my own one day. I first knew I wanted to be a dentist in high school. I volunteered at an occupational clinic full of doctors and surgeons. The staff pushed me to pursue a career in the medical field. When our clinic had an extension dental clinic constructed, I had the opportunity to work with dentists who were very passionate about their careers and convinced me that this was the work I wanted to do.

I currently work as a student researcher at the USDA Salinity Lab in Riverside, California. My job focuses on planting and maintaining crops as well as harvesting those crops for further studies. My experiment requires us to measure the salinity level/tolerance of a range of plant species by using differentiated salt levels to water the plants. During the plants growth cycle, my job is to frequently examine the growing process and take note of plants whose growth patterns deviate from normal. For example, if a plant seems to be growing at a faster rate than the other plants I will pay special attention to its ability to withstand increased salt levels in the soil. The plants we typically work with are vegetables so another way that we look at the plants salt tolerance is by comparing the total growth and net vegetable production between species during our harvest. Working in a lab setting has made me very curious and eager to learn new things. Doing research allows me to feel like a contributor to society and the lab setting has kept me engaged with my studies throughout undergrad.

I like to stay active in the community. I do this by volunteering my time at different locations in the Riverside area. One of the places I volunteer is Feeding America, which is a local food bank that collects and distributes donated food to the underprivileged community in our area. At the food bank, my job is to help organize and dispose of donated food to prepare several personalized care packages for redistribution. I also am a Dental Assistant volunteer at Smile Unto Him dental clinic. SUH is a non-profit dental clinic that provides free dental care to qualifying Riverside residents. Qualified patients must be 18 years of age or older, have no form of dental insurance. Volunteering my time at this clinic has made me realize that I enjoy helping the underserved community. The experiences I have made here have taught me that I am very passionate about pursuing a career in dentistry.
